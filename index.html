With a master's degree in psychology and my ongoing pursuit of a PGDCPCS(Post Graduate Diploma in Counseling Psychology and Counseling Skills), I am committed to making a positive impact on people's lives.

My Mission

My mission is to help individuals navigate the complex journey of life, particularly in the areas of relationships, education, and career. I believe in the power of counseling to bring clarity, resilience, and fulfillment to people's lives.

My Approach

My counseling approach is client-centered, compassionate, and evidence-based. I tailor my methods to suit the unique needs of each individual, ensuring that every counseling session is a collaborative journey toward personal growth and fulfillment.

What you get with Ritu Shriwastava's Counseling Services

Career Counseling

I assist individuals in identifying their strengths, passions, and career goals. Through personalized guidance, I help clients make informed career choices and navigate the path to professional success.

Relationship Counseling

I provide a safe and empathetic space for individuals and couples to work through their relationship challenges, improve communication, and build stronger, more meaningful connections.

Educational Counseling

Whether you're a student, parent, or educator, I offer guidance on educational choices, academic challenges, and creating effective learning strategies.
